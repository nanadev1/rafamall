Everything you need to know about RafaDoc consultations

What conditions can a medical herbalist help with?

Our herbalists specialize in various areas including stress and anxiety, digestive issues, sleep disorders, hormonal imbalances, immune support, skin conditions, and general wellness optimization.

How do I prepare for my consultation?

Complete your health history form, list any medications you're taking, prepare questions you have, and have your health goals in mind. This helps your herbalist provide the best possible care.

Is herbal medicine safe with my current medications?

Our herbalists are trained in herb-drug interactions. They will review your current medications and ensure any recommendations are safe and appropriate for your situation.

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your consultation, we'll provide a full refund or a complimentary follow-up session.
