Plant of the Month

Moringa Oleifera

Local Names: Zogale (Hausa), Gawara (Hausa), Ewe Igbale (Yoruba)

Native to: Ghana, Nigeria, West Africa
Harvest Season: Year-round
Traditional Uses: Immune support, nutrition, anti-inflammatory

The "Miracle Tree" is one of Africa's most valuable medicinal plants. Every part of the moringa tree has healing properties - leaves, seeds, bark, and roots. Rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.
