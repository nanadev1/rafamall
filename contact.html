Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee. Free shipping on orders over $50.

Are your products organic?

Yes! All our herbal products are sourced from certified organic farms. We work exclusively with partners who follow organic farming practices and avoid synthetic pesticides and fertilizers.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you're not completely satisfied, simply contact us for a full refund or exchange.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by location (5-14 business days). Customs fees may apply depending on your country.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order in your account dashboard.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for retailers, spas, and wellness centers. Please contact our wholesale team at wholesale@rafamall.com for a custom quote.
